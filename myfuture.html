    While Studying

    I believe I will study computer science diligently until I graduate, while balancing my studies and my personal life. I will study hard, but I will not neglect my life outside of the univercity. I will have friends, a social life, and opportunities to experience new things. I believe I can achieve a good balance between studying and living. I will strive to complete my degree within four years, making the most of every experience the university offers. I will also look for opportunities to work after graduating from computer science.

    After Graduation

    I think that if I can graduate in computer science, after completing my bachelor's degree, I will work in the field of computer science for at least two years. If I see that the work I am doing has potential for growth in the future and I truly enjoy this field, I believe I will pursue a master's degree in computer science to benefit my career. However, if after graduating and working for two years I do not see any opportunities for advancement in this profession or I do not enjoy this line of work, I may start a new bachelor's degree to change my career path.
